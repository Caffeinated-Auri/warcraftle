Warcraftle

A Worldle-based game for WoW

How To Play Warcraftle

Guess the word in six tries.

  • Each of your guesses must be a valid 5-letter word.
  • Only World of Warcraft-based words can be guessed.
  • The color of the tiles change to indicate how close your guess was.

Examples

W R A T H

W is in the word and in the correct position.

G U I L D

U is in the word and in the wrong position.

M A G N I

I is not in the current word.
